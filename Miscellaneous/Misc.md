[National Services Portal](https://services.india.gov.in/?ln=en) - This is the Government Services Portal of India, developed with an objective to enable a single window access to services being provided by the various Indian Government entities. 

[eCourt India Services](https://services.india.gov.in/service/detail/ecourt-india-services) - an online service that allows users to check case status, access and download court orders, and view judicial information using details like case number, party name, or advocate name, improving transparency and accessibility of court proceedings.

[Mo Dukan](https://modukan.in/) - a free website for odisha govt schemes, court judges, business websites.

[BareMinLaw](https://bareminlaw.com/) - an online legal resource decoding bareact and providing simplified access to bare acts, legal provisions, and study materials for Indian law students and professionals. It focuses on presenting legal content in an easy-to-understand format to aid quick reference and learning. 

[LegalDocs](https://legaldocs-tau.vercel.app/) - an online tool that allows users to generate professionally crafted legal documents instantly by entering their details. It offers customizable templates, state-specific compliance, and quick download options to simplify legal documentation processes.
