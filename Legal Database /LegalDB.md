[Indian Kanoon](https://indiankanoon.org/) - search engine for Indian law that helps users find laws and court judgments easily.

[Leegle](https://leegle.pages.dev/) - an AI-powered legal search and research tool designed to help users quickly find relevant case laws and legal information. It focuses on simplifying legal research through intelligent querying and streamlined access to judicial data. 

[Devgan.in](https://devgan.in/) - a mobile-friendly legal reference website providing Indian legislative acts, bare acts, Court Judgements and general legal information, designed especially for advocates and lawyers.
 
[TheLexBot](https://thelexbot.com/) - an AI-powered legal research platform that helps users quickly retrieve relevant judgments and laws with high precision and efficiency. It enables lawyers, students, and professionals to access legal insights, summaries, and case law through intelligent search across Indian judicial records.

[NJDG (National Judicial Data Grid)](https://njdg.ecourts.gov.in/njdg_v3/) - a national repository of case data providing consolidated statistics on cases instituted, disposed, and pending across High Courts and District Courts in India. It offers real-time, searchable judicial data and analytics to monitor case pendency and support transparency and decision-making in the judiciary. 

[eCourts Services](https://ecourts.gov.in/) - a government portal providing access to e-Courts services such as case status, cause lists, court orders/judgments, and the National Judicial Data Grid, helping litigants and lawyers track and manage cases online. 

[eCourts Judgments (eSCR)](https://judgments.ecourts.gov.in/pdfsearch/index.php) - a searchable platform for Indian judiciary judgments and orders where users can enter keywords, acts, or free text to find specific court decisions and access them in PDF format. 

[eCourtsIndia](https://ecourtsindia.com/) - an AI-powered legal information platform that provides comprehensive access to Indian court data, including case records, judgments, cause lists, lawyer directories, and litigant search. It enables users to search, track proceedings, and receive real-time updates using advanced AI-driven insights across courts in India.

[Legal Data Hunter](https://legaldatahunter.com/) - an open data legal platform that indexes legal sources worldwide through an interactive dashboard, tracking progress across multiple countries and datasets. It aims to build a global, multi-jurisdiction legal database by collecting, organizing, and making legal documents accessible for research and AI applications.
